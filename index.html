Instructions
1. Select a card that has the same number or sign with the card in the center. Double-click on it.
2. If you do not have a matching card, draw one from the right stack.
3. If one player shows a number 2 card, the other one will take two cards. If he also has a 2, the first player will take 4 cards.
4. Card number 3 has a similar role. If one player shows a 3-card, the other one will take three cards. If he also has a 3, the first player will take 9 cards.
5. The one who finishes his cards first wins the game
